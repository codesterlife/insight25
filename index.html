About Us

Insight, the prestigious national IT fest by CCSIT CU Campus, has fostered technocrats and opportunities for over 20 years. Now in its 22nd edition, it showcases cutting-edge technology, engaging discussions, workshops, and tech games, attracting enthusiasts nationwide. Held at Kerala's largest university, Insight celebrates innovation and creativity, inspiring transformative ideas while expanding its legacy.

F.A.Q

  • What are the general guidelines for participants of Insight'25

    ● Registration for events and seminars will primarily be conducted online. However, spot registrations will also be available as a secondary option. Please note that spot registrations will not be available for the Paper Presentation and Cultural Events.

    ● Any form of unethical behavior or violation of the rules will result in the immediate disqualification of the participants.

    ● Participants must report 30 minutes before each event, and a valid college ID card is mandatory for participation.

    ● The winners of each event will be determined by the judges' decisions.

    ● Teams or participants who fail to report to the venue on time will be disqualified.

    ● A minimum of 4 participants is required for individual events, and a minimum of 4 teams is required for team events. Events failing to meet these criteria will be cancelled.

    ● Registration fees will only be refunded for cancelled events; otherwise, they are non-refundable.

    ● The winners of each event will receive certificates and cash prizes, while all participants will be awarded participation certificates.

    ● The final decisions made by the committee are final and will not be subject to change.

    ● Accommodation will be provided exclusively to participants who register online for both days.

  • How do I register for the tech fest?

    Registrations are completely event based. Please visit individual event pages for more info.

  • Are there any prerequisites or requirements for the seminar or competitions?

    Any kind of details regarding the prerequisites for events will be provided in the event guidelines section for every event. Please read them carefully before registering for the event.

  • Where will the tech fest be held?

    Insight'25 is happening at Calicut University Campus, Thenhipalam, Malappuram. A linktree for all the locations within the campus where the individual events are hosted are provided at the venue information above.

  • Will food or refreshments be provided?

    Details regarding providing of food and refreshments for participants will be announced later. We'll update all registered participants on the matter directly and will make announcements on the website also. Please check the updates section on the website for these annoucements.

  • How can I contact the organizers if I have more questions?

    For any doubts or questions regarding the tech fest in general, please contact the listed phone numbers below or via email. For event specific enquiries please contact the individual event organizers directly through the numbers provided in the event posters or ticket registration page.
