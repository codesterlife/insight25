About Us

Insight, the prestigious national IT fest by CCSIT CU Campus, has fostered technocrats and opportunities for over 20 years. Now in its 22nd edition, it showcases cutting-edge technology, engaging discussions, workshops, and tech games, attracting enthusiasts nationwide. Held at Kerala's largest university, Insight celebrates innovation and creativity, inspiring transformative ideas while expanding its legacy.
