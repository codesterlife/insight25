Event Guidelines

  • Open to all individual participants.
  • Each participant can submit only one photograph.
  • Only mobile phone cameras are allowed; other types of cameras are not permitted.
  • Photographs must be submitted in high-resolution JPEG format.
  • No watermarks or signatures are allowed on the images.
  • A clearly defined theme will be provided for each competition.
  • Entries must align with the specified theme.
  • Submitted photographs must be the original work of the participant.
  • Participants retain full copyright of their images.
  • The use of stock photos or copyrighted material is strictly prohibited.
  • Basic adjustments (e.g., color correction, cropping) are allowed.
  • Excessive digital manipulation that alters the original content is prohibited.
  • Judging criteria include creativity, composition, relevance to the theme, and technical skill.
  • Inappropriate or offensive content will lead to disqualification.
  • Entries that fail to adhere to the rules and regulations may be disqualified.
  • The decision of the judges is final and cannot be contested.
  • For any reason a person did not attend the event after ticket registration, the ticket amount WILL NOT BE REFUNDED.
