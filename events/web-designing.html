Event Guidelines

  • This is an individual event.
  • Multiple participants from the same college are encouraged.
  • Participants have the option to bring their personal laptops. However, if they do not have one, necessary systems will be provided.
  • The topic and requirements will be provided at the time of the competition.
  • Participants must create an attractive website for the given topic using HTML and CSS within one hour.
  • Participants are only allowed to use Notepad as an IDE for the event.
  • Templates are not allowed to be used.
  • Images will be provided at the time of the competition if needed.
  • If the number of registrations exceeds the limit, prelims may be conducted.
  • The decision of the judges will be final.
  • A minimum of 4 participant is required for team event. Event failing to meet these criteria will be cancelled
  • For any reason a person did not attend the event after ticket registration, the ticket amount WILL NOT BE REFUNDED.
