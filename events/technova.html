Event Guidelines

  • The main attraction of Insight is TECHNOVA, an event designed to identify the best geek. It consists of five rounds: Initium, Techsense, Intersepto, Extremist, and Jackpot.
  • Each level will consist of multiple tasks with varying degrees of difficulty.
  • Participating teams are required to report to the venue at least 30 minutes prior to the scheduled start time.
  • All participating teams must bring at least one laptop with them.
  • only team participation is permitted
  • Each team may have a maximum of five members.
  • Each team will be assigned one or more volunteers by the TECHNOVA core team.
  • An elimination stage will take place at a specific point in the game.
  • If the number of registrations exceeds the set limit, a preliminary round may be conducted.
  • All final decisions will be made by the TECHNOVA core team, and no further queries or appeals regarding these decisions will be entertained.
  • Internet access will be provided in varying capacities based on the participants requirements.
  • Any malpractice detected during the event will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Participants are required to have basic knowledge of at least one programming language, such as C, C++, Python, Java, PHP, or others.
  • The Jackpot prize of 25,000 will be awarded to the participants who crack all the levels first.
  • Participants may request a limited number of clues from their assigned volunteers if they get stuck on any particular task.
  • The use of any browser extensions is strictly prohibited.
  • A minimum of 4 team is required for team event. Event failing to meet these criteria will be cancelled
  • For any reason a person/team did not attend the event after ticket registration, the ticket amount WILL NOT BE REFUNDED.
