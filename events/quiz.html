Event Guidelines

  • Rules:

    • A team of a maximum of 2 members will be allowed.
    • Any number of teams can participate from the same college.
    • The top 4 teams qualifying in the prelims will compete in further rounds.
    • There will be questions for the audience, and attractive prizes will be distributed.

  • Rounds:

    • In the final round, the top team will be selected as the winner.
    • The decision of the quiz master will be final, and no further queries will be entertained.
    • Any form of unethical practice or violation of the rules will result in the disqualification of the team.
  • For any reason a person/team did not attend the event after ticket registration, the ticket amount WILL NOT BE REFUNDED.
